Meet the Farmer

Bethel Okonji

Meet Bethel Okonji — a passionate beekeeper and farmer from Eastern Nigeria. His deep love for the land and respect for nature drove him to build something truly pure: Betey Honey.

Every jar comes straight from his carefully managed hives — raw, unfiltered, and completely free from additives. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just honey the way nature intended.

What sets Bethel apart is his rare combination of traditional farming wisdom and modern technology — always seeking better, smarter ways to serve nature and his customers.
